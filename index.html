CSV Format Guide

English CSV columns:
q_no, question_en, option_a, option_b, option_c, option_d, answer

Malayalam CSV columns:
q_no, question_ml, option_a, option_b, option_c, option_d, answer

The answer column should be A, B, C, or D. Upload both or either — they'll be merged by q_no. Malayalam options override English options when both are uploaded.
